Early Detection of Chronic Kidney Disease using Machine Learning




About:

CKD is a condition in which the kidneys are damaged and cannot filter blood as well as they should. CKD has varying levels of seriousness. It usually gets worse over time. If left untreated, CKD can progress to kidney failure and early cardiovascular disease. When the kidneys stop working, dialysis or kidney transplant is needed for survival

Symptoms:

  • hypertension
  • edema
  • fatigue
  • decreased urine output
  • loss of appetite
